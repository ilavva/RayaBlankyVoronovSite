Article: The “Ensemble”—A Group Music Therapy Treatment for Developing Preschool Children’s Social Skills
Published in: ”International Journal of Environmental Research and Public Health” on 2 August 2022
DOI:   https://doi.org/10.3390/ijerph19159446

Several music therapy treatments have been developed to assist children with social skill deficiencies. They lack reference to emotions and their connection to social skills and they don’t deal with group dynamics and its impact on the group. We conducted a preliminary examination of the “Ensemble” treatment, which was developed to improve the social skills of children of various client populations, with the social deficiency originating from different sources.
